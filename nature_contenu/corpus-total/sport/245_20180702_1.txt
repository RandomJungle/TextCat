GREAT MONUMENTS OF TOULOUSE (SCIENCE IN THE CITY) à Toulouse Visit proposed by the tourist office. For an initial discovery of Toulouse, this tour offers an overview of the main sites and monuments. During the tour, you walk through the town, have a look at the town hall square, the Saint-Sernin basilica, the Jacobins Convent and wonderful views along the river Garonne. Meeting point : tourist office, donjon du Capitole at 2.30 pm.
